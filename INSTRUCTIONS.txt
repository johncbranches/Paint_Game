Instructions:

Move: Use the arrow keys
Draw: Move while keeping the space-bar pressed
Erase: Move over the drawn fields while pressing the space-bar
Change color: Press the "m" key 
Save: Press the "s" key  (Will save the current drawn fields)
Load: Press the "l" key  (Will load the last saved configuration of th drawn fields)
Clear: Press the "c" key
Exit: Press the "e" key or close the window
